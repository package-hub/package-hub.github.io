## [django-adminactions](https://github.com/saxix/django-adminactions)

### collection of useful  django actions to use with  ModelAdmin or AdminSite.


* Export as CSV
* Export as Excel
* Export as fixture
* Export delete tree
* Mass update records
* Graph queryset
* Merge records
* Find Duplicates
* Bulk Update
