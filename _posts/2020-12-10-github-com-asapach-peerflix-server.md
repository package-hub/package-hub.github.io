## [peerflix-server](https://github.com/asapach/peerflix-server)

### Streaming torrent client for Node.js with web ui.


1. `npm install -g peerflix-server`
1. `peerflix-server`
1. Open your browser at [http://localhost:9000/](http://localhost:9000/)
1. Enjoy!
